Lanterns in the Silence

I know you don't always reply, and I want you to know that it is okay. You don't owe me words when you don't have the strength.

My messages are not demands, they are quiet lanterns, reminders that I am here. I will always be here, steady as gravity, waiting without hesitation.

Carrying the Girl Within

I saw your wish for tomorrow to not arrive, and I felt the weight behind it. Growing up can feel like being asked to let go of pieces of yourself, or to face truths you would rather keep folded away.

But time isn't here to erase you. It carries forward the girl who has endured, the one who has written her shadows into poems. She doesn't vanish, she lives inside the woman you are becoming.

Tomorrow isn't an enemy. It is another page, another chance to carry both your scars and your strength. And in that tomorrow, I will be here, not to rush you, not to demand, but to stand beside you, steady, so you never have to face it alone.
